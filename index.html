Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2025

1. Overview

Omega Connect ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

We are designed to be a privacy-first platform. Here is what we do and do not collect:

  • No personal data — We do not require registration, email, or phone number
  • No IP tracking — We do not log or store your IP address
  • No cookies for tracking — We only use essential cookies for functionality
  • Anonymous identifiers — Temporary session IDs are generated and discarded after use
  • Chat data — Messages are temporary and deleted from our servers after the chat session ends

3. Local Storage

We may store minimal preferences (theme, interests) in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

Chat messages and session data are automatically deleted when a chat session ends. We do not maintain any archives or backups of user conversations.

Omega Connect is strictly for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 18.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, there is minimal data to manage. If you have any concerns, please contact us.
